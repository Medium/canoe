complete()

Finishes a multi-part upload. Any remaning data is flushed from the underlying queue. Once all parts have been uploaded to S3 and the 'flush' event is fired, completion starts. The stream emits a 'complete' event when the completed upload finishes with `err` and `response` arguments.
